Installed

The native host was registered for the current user.

Final steps:

  1. Fully quit and reopen your browser (Quit from the menu — closing the window isn't enough).
  2. Make sure the Discord desktop app is running.
  3. Play a YouTube or YouTube Music tab — your Discord presence should appear.

To uninstall: run uninstall.sh from /Library/Application Support/YouTubeDiscordPresence/, then delete that folder.
